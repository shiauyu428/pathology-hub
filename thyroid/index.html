Reference Notes

1. Criteria for Angioinvasion
Papillary thyroid carcinomas tend to spread via lymphatics. In addition to tumor deposits within lymphatic spaces, this form of spread may manifest as psammoma bodies alone within these spaces, which are the equivalent of lymphatic invasion for reporting purposes.

2. Psammoma bodies in lymph nodes
Reporting of "psammoma bodies only" in lymph nodes is not well defined. While indolent, they do indicate capacity for lymphatic spread and are considered pN1a.
